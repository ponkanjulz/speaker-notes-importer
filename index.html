Speaker Notes Importer

Speaker Notes Importer is a Google Slides add-on that allows users to easily import speaker notes into slides.

About

This add-on helps presenters quickly transfer notes into Google Slides presentations. Speaker Notes Importer is a Google Slides add-on that allows users to paste structured text and automatically insert it into slide speaker notes. The add-on reads text formatted with slide labels such as “Slide 1”, “Slide 2”, and similar headings. Each section is mapped to the corresponding slide and inserted into that slide’s speaker notes. This helps users quickly populate presentation notes without manually copying text slide by slide.

Privacy Policy

This application does not collect or store personal data. All processing happens inside the user's Google account. https://sites.google.com/view/speaker-notes-privacy/home
